Rule

CODEGATE 2017 CTF RULE

1. Preliminary competition schedule (Date : UTC+9)
1. General teams: February 10th (Friday) 9:00am – 11th (Saturday), 2017, 24hours
2. University teams : February 10th (Friday) 9:00am – 11th (Saturday), 2017, 24hours
3. Junior teams : February 10th (Friday), 2017 9:00am – 9:00pm, 12 hours

2. Application
1. You are able to apply until February 10th, 2017, 08:59 (UTC+9).
2. Please make sure that you enter the information of participant when registering for the preliminary competition.
3. Those going into the final competition of general teams and university teams shall be selected among the 10 members registered for the preliminary competition (you may change this on your team page after team registration.)
4. Multiple participation in the final competitions of general team and university team is prohibited.

3. Ranking decision
1. Hacking defense competition comprises competitions of general, university and junior teams. The preliminary competition will be conducted online, and the final competition for general (10 teams), university students (10 teams), junior (30 peoples) will take place offline.
2. If any teams give up after the preliminary competition or cheatings are identified, next highest ranking team goes into the final.
3. There is no fixed format for a write-up. The write-up should be submitted to the organizer (codegate2017@gmail.com) within 3 days after the end of the competition ( until February 14th, 2017, 23:59(UTC+9) )
4. Those passed the preliminary competition of university teams and junior teams should submit a student identification card or a certificate of studentship so as to verify their studentship.

4. Cheating
1. If any acts of sharing problem solving methods or answers are identified, all members of the relevant team may be banned from participating in the competition.
2. Those who interrupt the competition by launching brute force attack, denial of service attack or by trying to infiltrate an unauthorized server will be disqualified.
3. Any teams violating the rules of the competition by doing such acts as cheating will be disqualified and details of such violation will be disclosed.

Matters not stipulated in this hacking competition rule shall be additionally determined by the operating committee of the competition.
